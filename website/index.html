Meet Kim Schlesinger

Hello! My name is Kim, and I grew up in Cañon City, Colorado. Below is a brief timeline about me, and my journey leading to gSchool.

Timeline

2008 ➜ I complete my undergraduate degree in music at the University of Northern Colorado, and I teach elementary school music for two years in Fort Collins, Colorado.

2010 ➜ I join Teach For America, move to Milwaukee, Wisconsin, and teach students with emotional/behavioral, and learning disabilities.

2012 ➜ I move back to Fort Collins, and teach special education for two more years.

2014 ➜ I attend Bikram Yoga Teacher Training in Khao Lak, Thailand. I return to Colorado, move to Boulder, and become a yoga teacher!

2015 ➜ I decide to attend gSchool.
